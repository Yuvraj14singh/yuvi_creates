01 · About me

The founder behind the build.

Every useful website starts with understanding the business—not choosing a template.

I'm Yuvraj Singh, the founder of Yuvi Creates, a BCA final-year student and a Python and Django developer who enjoys turning real business needs into thoughtful digital products.

As a full-stack web developer, I build modern business websites, focused landing pages and web applications. My work combines responsive design, API integration and UI-focused development, but the process never begins with a list of technologies. It begins with a conversation.

“I don't build websites based on my own preferences. Every business is different, so I first understand the client's goals, audience, requirements and vision before designing anything.”

A website should feel like the business it represents. If I am building for a salon, the online experience should carry the same sense of care, style and atmosphere that a customer feels when visiting the salon in person. That consistency helps people recognise the business and trust what they see.

When a business needs more control, I also develop custom admin panels for managing images, content, enquiries, business records and other custom data. These panels follow the client's actual workflow instead of forcing every business into the same generic dashboard.

Two projects reflect this approach clearly. PitchQI is an AI-assisted cricket intelligence platform built with Django, bringing live scorecards, teams, players, match statistics and API-powered cricket data into one responsive web application. Yuvi Creates is where I design custom websites, landing pages and business-focused digital solutions for different industries.

I am still learning, improving my development skills and testing better ways to build. The goal is simple: create practical solutions that are clear to use and solve a real business problem.
