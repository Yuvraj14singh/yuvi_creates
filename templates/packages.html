Common questions

Before we start

Clear answers about pricing, timelines and working together.

How much will my website cost?

Every project is quoted individually. Final pricing depends on the number of pages, design complexity, required functionality, integrations, content, timeline, and support requirements.

Which currencies do you accept?

Proposals can be prepared in INR, USD, GBP, AUD, CAD, or another mutually agreed currency.

Do you charge the same amount in every country?

Pricing is based on the project requirements and scope, not automatic currency conversion. The final proposal confirms the deliverables, timeline, currency, and payment schedule before work begins.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Projects can be handled remotely for clients in India and internationally through email, online meetings, milestone reviews, and digital delivery.

Can I request features that are not included in a package?

Yes. Additional features can be included in a custom proposal after the functionality and technical requirements are reviewed.

Do you provide domain and hosting?

Setup support is available. Domain, hosting, paid tools and third-party services are quoted separately unless explicitly included in the proposal.

How long does a website project take?

Timelines vary based on scope, content readiness and feedback speed. A practical delivery timeline is confirmed in the proposal.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes. The current website can be reviewed and a redesign scope prepared around usability, presentation, responsiveness and performance needs.

Do you provide support after launch?

Launch support is included as defined in the proposal. Ongoing updates or maintenance can be discussed separately.

What do I need to provide before the project starts?

Usually your business details, services, available brand assets, content, images and required functionality. Exact requirements are shared before development.
